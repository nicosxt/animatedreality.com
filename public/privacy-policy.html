Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-02-05

Welcome to Everything is Alive. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when using our application on Meta Quest.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal user data. However, our app integrates with a third-party service, Monaverse, for optional authentication and asset downloads.

  • Monaverse Login (Optional): Users may choose to log in with their Monaverse.com account. This authentication is handled entirely by Monaverse, and we do not store or process any user credentials or personal data within our app.
  • Downloading 3D Objects: If you choose to access 3D objects from Monaverse, those assets are downloaded directly to your local device and are not stored on any external database or server operated by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We do not collect or use your information in any way.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any personal data to third parties. Any authentication or data retrieval is handled directly by Monaverse through their own API, subject to their Terms of Service.

4. User Rights & Choices

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no data for users to access, modify, or request deletion of.

5. Security Measures

As we do not store any user data, there are no additional security risks related to personal data.
